What We Do

SAP ECC & S/4 HANA Rollouts

A SAP rollout is the process of extending an already-implemented SAP system to additional organizational entities — whether that's a new country, subsidiary, business unit, or plant. Rather than re-implementing from scratch, rollouts leverage your existing template and adapt it to local requirements.

Jigsutech specializes in both SAP ECC rollouts and the newer S/4 HANA rollouts, helping global enterprises standardize their SAP landscape while accommodating local legal, tax, and regulatory requirements efficiently.

  • Country-specific legal and tax compliance configuration
  • Template-based rollout for faster deployment
  • Data migration from legacy systems to SAP
  • Local language and currency configuration
  • Integration with regional third-party systems
  • End-user training and go-live support

Rollout Types

Types of SAP Rollouts We Handle

Every rollout engagement is unique. We bring expertise across all rollout scenarios and scales.

01

Country / Regional Rollout

Deploy SAP to a new country while adapting the global template for local statutory requirements — GST, VAT, payroll, statutory reports, and local chart of accounts.

02

Subsidiary Rollout

Bring newly acquired or existing subsidiaries onto the central SAP system — standardizing processes while preserving any entity-specific customizations needed.

03

Plant / Site Rollout

Extend SAP to new manufacturing plants, warehouses, or distribution centers — configuring plant-specific master data, production processes, and logistics flows.

04

Module Extension Rollout

Roll out additional SAP modules (e.g. SAP PM, QM, TM) to existing entities that are already on SAP but using only a subset of modules.

05

ECC to S/4 HANA Rollout

For companies mid-migration — roll out S/4 HANA to new entities while older entities are still on ECC, managing a hybrid landscape during transition.

06

Post-Merger Integration Rollout

After M&A activity, rapidly onboard the acquired entity's operations onto the parent's SAP landscape — maintaining business continuity throughout.

Our Methodology

Rollout Implementation Phases

Jigsutech follows a structured, template-driven rollout approach that minimizes risk and accelerates deployment.

1
Template Assessment & Fit-Gap Analysis

Review the existing global SAP template against the new entity's requirements. Identify gaps where local legal, tax, or process requirements deviate from the standard template and define a plan to address them.

2
Organizational Structure & Master Data Setup

Configure the new company code, plant, sales org, and other organizational entities. Set up chart of accounts, local tax codes, currency, language, and all relevant master data including customers, vendors, and materials.

3
Local Configuration & Custom Development

Configure country-specific legal requirements — local statutory reports, e-invoicing (GST/VAT), payroll localization, and any required custom ABAP development for local business processes not covered by the template.

4
Integration Setup & Data Migration

Configure integrations with local banks, tax authorities, and third-party systems. Migrate historical data from legacy systems including open items, balances, and master data using SAP migration tools.

5
Testing — SIT, UAT & Regression

Execute system integration testing, user acceptance testing with local business teams, and regression testing to ensure the rollout hasn't impacted any existing entities on the shared landscape.

6
Go-Live, Cutover & Hypercare

Execute the cutover plan — migrate final open items, perform balance confirmation, and go live. Provide intensive hypercare support for the first weeks post go-live to ensure smooth adoption and resolve any issues rapidly.
